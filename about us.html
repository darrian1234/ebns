Managing Director

Mr. Roger Talayaratne

Master's in Business Management | Certified Management Accountant

Mr. Roger Talayaratne holds a Master's Degree in Business Management, majoring in Marketing and Human resource management from the Manipal University, India. He is also a certified management accountant from the Institute of Certified Management Accountants, and a certified marketer with 27 years of experience in sales, distribution, marketing and business strategy.

He is currently the Managing Director of Ebenezer Double Edged Solutions Pvt Ltd, Non-Executive Director at Pership Holdings Group of Companies and the Vice President Administration for the Board of Management of the Management Club Sri Lanka, and the immediate past Chairman of The Management Club Mt Lavinia branch.
